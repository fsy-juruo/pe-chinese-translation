
### [296. Angular Bisector and Tangent](https://projecteuler.net/problem=296)

Given is an integer sided triangle $ABC$ with $BC \le AC \le AB$.  
$k$ is the angular bisector of angle $ACB$.  
$m$ is the tangent at $C$ to the circumscribed circle of $ABC$.  
$n$ is a line parallel to $m$ through $B$.  
The intersection of $n$ and $k$ is called $E$.

![](images/0296_bisector.gif)

How many triangles $ABC$ with a perimeter not exceeding $100\,000$ exist such that $BE$ has integral length?

### 296. 角平分线和切线

给定一个满足 $BC \le AC \le AB$ 的、各边长都是整数的三角形 $ABC$。$k$ 是 $\angle ACB$ 的角平分线，$m$ 是 $\triangle ABC$ 的外接圆在 $C$ 处的切线。过 $B$ 作 $m$ 的平行线，记其为 $n$，并记 $n, k$ 的交点为 $E$。

![](images/0296_bisector.gif)

满足上述条件，周长不超过 $100\,000$ 且 $BE$ 长度是整数的三角形有多少个？
